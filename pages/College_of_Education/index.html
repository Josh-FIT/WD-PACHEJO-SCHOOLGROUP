College of Education

Equipping future educators with knowledge, skills, and passion to inspire students.

Programs Offered

Bachelor of Secondary Education major in English

The Bachelor of Secondary Education major in English prepares students to become skilled educators in the field of language and literature. The program focuses on English literacy, literature analysis, and teaching methodologies designed to enhance students’ communication and critical thinking skills.

Program Educational Objectives

  • Equip students with a deep understanding of English language and literature.
  • Prepare graduates to teach English effectively at the secondary school level.
  • Promote student-centered learning and foster inclusivity in English language education.

Program Outcomes

  • Design curriculum and lessons that engage students in both literary and language studies.
  • Utilize effective teaching techniques to improve English language proficiency among learners.
  • Assess student writing and oral communication skills, providing constructive feedback.
  • Foster critical thinking and creativity through diverse literary approaches.
  • Maintain a classroom environment conducive to effective learning and development.

Bachelor of Secondary Education major in Mathematics

The BSEd major in Mathematics program provides students with a strong foundation in mathematical concepts and teaching techniques. This program prepares future educators to teach mathematics at the secondary level, focusing on problem-solving, analytical thinking, and effective math instruction.

Program Educational Objectives

  • Provide students with the knowledge and skills to teach mathematics at the secondary school level.
  • Promote critical thinking and problem-solving abilities in students.
  • Prepare graduates to engage students in meaningful mathematical inquiry and learning.

Program Outcomes

  • Develop and implement lesson plans that enhance mathematical understanding.
  • Utilize teaching strategies that foster critical thinking and engagement in mathematics.
  • Assess student progress through various methods, including tests, projects, and class participation.
  • Maintain a positive and motivating classroom environment for effective learning.
  • Engage in continuous professional development to stay current with educational best practices.

Bachelor of Secondary Education major in Science (Boni Campus)

The BSEd major in Science program at Boni Campus is designed for students who are passionate about teaching scientific subjects at the secondary level. The program covers a broad range of scientific disciplines, including biology, chemistry, and physics, while emphasizing inquiry-based teaching methods.

Program Educational Objectives

  • Prepare students to teach science in secondary schools with a focus on inquiry-based learning.
  • Equip students with both subject knowledge and pedagogical skills for teaching science effectively.
  • Foster an appreciation of scientific methods and critical thinking in students.

Program Outcomes

  • Design and implement lessons that encourage scientific inquiry and experimentation.
  • Use diverse teaching strategies to effectively engage students in various branches of science.
  • Provide feedback on students’ scientific understanding through formative and summative assessments.
  • Incorporate technology and laboratory resources to enhance the learning of scientific concepts.
  • Stay updated with the latest developments in science education through professional development opportunities.
