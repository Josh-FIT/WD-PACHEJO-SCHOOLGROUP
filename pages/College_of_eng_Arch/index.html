Programs Offered

Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (BSME)

The BSME program at Rizal Technological University equips students with the skills and knowledge to excel in the fields of mechanical engineering. Graduates are expected to pursue careers in mechanical system design, manufacturing, maintenance, and other related areas.

Program Educational Objectives

  • Possess the knowledge and abilities to pursue positions in mechanical design, installation, manufacturing, testing, technical sales, and maintenance.
  • Engage in advanced education, research and development, and industrial training in science and technology.
  • Provide solutions and solve problems related to mechanical engineering technologies.

Program Outcomes

  • Apply knowledge of mathematics and science to solve complex mechanical engineering problems.
  • Conduct experiments, as well as analyze and interpret data.
  • Design systems, components, or processes to meet realistic constraints in accordance with standards.
  • Function effectively in multidisciplinary and multi-cultural teams.
  • Identify, formulate, and solve complex mechanical engineering problems.
  • Understand professional and ethical responsibilities.
  • Communicate effectively in written and oral forms.
  • Evaluate the impact of engineering solutions in global, economic, environmental, and social contexts.
  • Engage in lifelong learning and recognize its importance in professional growth.
  • Use modern engineering tools, techniques, and skills essential for mechanical engineering practices.

Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (BSCE)

  • Achieve a high level of technical expertise to succeed in the Civil Engineering profession.
  • Engage in lifelong learning activities such as leadership roles in applied research and development studies in engineering and technology.
  • Provide professional engineering services to assist in technology transfer and contribute to the development of small and medium-scale industries for national development.

Program Outcomes

  • Apply knowledge of mathematics and science to solve complex civil engineering problems.
  • Design and conduct experiments, as well as analyze and interpret data.
  • Design a system, component, or process to meet desired needs within realistic constraints, such as economic, environmental, social, ethical, health and safety, manufacturability, and sustainability, in accordance with standards.
  • Function effectively in multidisciplinary and multi-cultural teams.
  • Identify, formulate, and solve complex civil engineering problems.
  • Understand professional and ethical responsibilities.
  • Communicate effectively with the engineering community and society at large.
  • Understand the impact of civil engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental, and societal context.
  • Recognize the need for and engage in lifelong learning.
  • Understand contemporary issues and challenges in civil engineering.
  • Use techniques, skills, and modern engineering tools necessary for civil engineering practice.
  • Apply engineering and management principles as a member and leader of a team, managing projects in a multidisciplinary environment.
  • Demonstrate understanding and proficiency in at least one specialized field of civil engineering practice.

Bachelor of Science in Architecture (BSArch)

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Science in Architecture (BSArch) program at Rizal Technological University is a comprehensive course that equips students with the skills and knowledge to excel in the field of architecture. With a focus on sustainable design, innovative practices, and technical expertise, the program prepares graduates to meet the challenges of modern architecture.

Program Educational Objectives

  • Develop proficiency in architectural design, construction technology, and project management.
  • Promote innovative and sustainable design solutions addressing environmental and societal needs.
  • Engage in lifelong learning through research, advanced studies, and professional development.

Program Outcomes

  • Apply principles of architectural design and engineering to create functional, aesthetic, and sustainable structures.
  • Utilize modern tools and technologies in drafting, modeling, and project presentations.
  • Analyze and solve architectural problems through creative and critical thinking.
  • Demonstrate professional and ethical responsibility in architectural practice.
  • Collaborate effectively within multidisciplinary teams in the built environment industry.
  • Communicate architectural concepts effectively to clients, stakeholders, and the public.
  • Understand the social, cultural, and environmental implications of architectural solutions.
  • Stay updated with global trends and innovations in the field of architecture.
