Programs Offered

Bachelor of Arts in Political Science

The Bachelor of Arts in Political Science program is designed to develop a deep understanding of political systems, governance, public policy, and international relations. Students will gain the knowledge and skills to critically analyze political events, engage in policy research, and contribute to public discourse.

Program Educational Objectives

  • Prepare graduates for careers in public service, advocacy, policy-making, and political research.
  • Equip students with a strong foundation in political analysis, critical thinking, and research methodologies.
  • Foster a commitment to ethical leadership, social responsibility, and active citizenship in a globalized world.

Program Outcomes

  • Analyze and interpret political events, trends, and policies using a range of political analysis tools.
  • Communicate effectively, both orally and in writing, in political and public affairs discussions.
  • Engage in policy advocacy, contribute to political discourse, and participate in community-driven initiatives to influence public opinion and policy.
  • Apply principles of ethical decision-making and responsible leadership in the context of public service and governance.

Bachelor of Science in Psychology

The Bachelor of Science in Psychology program is designed to provide students with an understanding of human behavior and mental processes. It focuses on areas like developmental psychology, counseling, social psychology, and research methodologies, preparing students for careers in mental health, human resources, and social services.

Program Educational Objectives

  • Prepare students for careers in psychology, counseling, human resources, and related fields.
  • Equip students with a thorough understanding of psychological theories, research methods, and ethical standards in practice.
  • Develop strong interpersonal skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities to apply psychological principles in various settings.

Program Outcomes

  • Apply psychological principles and research methods to understand behavior and address psychological issues.
  • Conduct psychological research, analyze data, and present findings clearly.
  • Provide effective communication and counseling strategies for individuals and groups.
  • Demonstrate professional ethics and responsibility in psychological practice.

Bachelor of Science in Statistics (Boni Campus)

The Bachelor of Science in Statistics program at Rizal Technological University equips students with a solid understanding of statistical theory and methodologies. Students are trained to analyze data, perform statistical modeling, and apply statistical techniques in various fields including economics, business, and social sciences.

Program Educational Objectives

  • Prepare students to apply statistical methods in research, industry, and other fields requiring data analysis.
  • Equip graduates with a strong foundation in probability theory, statistical inference, and statistical computing.
  • Develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills, enabling students to address complex data-related challenges.

Program Outcomes

  • Apply statistical principles and methods to solve real-world problems across different domains such as business, healthcare, and government.
  • Communicate statistical findings clearly and effectively to both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Demonstrate proficiency in statistical software and other data analysis tools.
  • Engage in continuous learning and stay updated with advancements in statistical methods and technologies.
